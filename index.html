Questions parents will ask

Use this section to reduce hesitation before checkout.

What age is Gaia’s Secret Agents for?

It is designed for children aged 6–11. Younger children may need more adult help. Older children can work more independently.

Do children need to live near the ocean?

No. Mission 01 is ocean-themed, but the activity and real-world action can be done from home. The Plastic-Free Lunchbox Challenge is designed to work anywhere.

Is this a box?

No. Gaia’s Secret Agents is designed as a flat monthly mail pack. It focuses on story, cards, activities, and printed mission materials rather than bulky items.

Is it educational?

Yes. Each mission has a parent-facing learning outcome. The child learns through story, investigation, hands-on activity, observation, and one small real-world action.

Does it require screens?

No. The core product is screen-free and physical. You can add optional digital extras later, but the main experience works through the mailed mission pack.

What does the child earn?

Each mission includes an earned badge and a featured Agent Super Power. In Mission 01, the badge is Ocean Discoverer and the Super Power is Curiosity.
