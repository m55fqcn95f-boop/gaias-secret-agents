FAQ: monthly nature mission packs for kids

Clear answers for parents before they subscribe.

What is Gaia’s Secret Agents?

Gaia’s Secret Agents is a screen-free monthly nature mission club for children aged 6–11. Each mission is sent as a physical snail-mail mission pack from Secret Headquarters.

What age is Gaia’s Secret Agents for?

It is designed for children aged 6–11. Younger children may need more adult help. Older children can work more independently.

Is this an environmental education subscription?

Yes. It teaches real nature and environmental ideas, but it is framed as a secret-agent mission club instead of a school worksheet or generic craft box.

Do children need to live near the ocean?

No. Mission 01 is ocean-themed, but the activity and real-world action can be completed from home. The Plastic-Free Lunchbox Challenge works anywhere.

Is this doom and gloom?

No. The tone is hopeful and practical. Children are not told the world is hopeless. They are given a role, a mission, and a small useful action they can complete.

What does the child earn?

Each mission includes an earned badge and a featured Agent Super Power. In Mission 01, the child earns the Ocean Discoverer badge and builds Curiosity.
